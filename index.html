Valuation Methodology

Revenue Multiple Approach

We applied a 23.7x forward revenue multiple on the projected 2026 revenue of $6.1B. This premium valuation is strongly justified by:

  • Massive TAM: The rapidly expanding commercial space economy.
  • Secured Contracts: Multi-billion dollar backlogs including Amazon's Project Kuiper (38 launches) and NASA's Artemis (Blue Moon lander).
  • Strategic Duopoly: Blue Origin is the only viable hedge against a SpaceX monopoly in heavy-lift launch, guaranteeing a steady stream of government defense and commercial payloads.

Risk Factors

⚠️

Technical & Execution Risk

Any further delays or launch failures with the New Glenn heavy-lift vehicle will push back major revenue realization timelines.

🎯

Customer Concentration

The company currently relies heavily on two major clients: NASA (Artemis) and Amazon (Kuiper). Loss of either would be catastrophic to revenue projections.

🚀

Competitive Squeeze

If SpaceX achieves full rapid reusability with Starship before New Glenn matures, launch market prices could crash faster than Blue Origin can achieve cost parity.
